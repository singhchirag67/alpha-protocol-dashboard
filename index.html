Addressing the Recessed Chin

At 14, your facial bones are still malleable. A recessed chin is often caused by poor tongue posture, mouth breathing, and forward head posture.

  • ✓ Proper Tongue Posture (Mewing): The entire tongue must rest on the roof of the mouth, applying gentle pressure to the maxilla. Keep lips sealed, breathe through the nose.
  • ✓ Chin Tucks: Stand against a wall and pull your head straight back, giving yourself a "double chin." Hold for 5 seconds. Do 10 reps daily to fix neck posture.
  • ✓ Chewing: Eat harder, whole foods (carrots, nuts) to develop the masseter muscles and stimulate jaw growth.

The Minimum Effective Dose

You don't need 2 hours in a gym. You need 15 minutes of extreme focus and high intensity.

This protocol uses bodyweight exercises taken close to muscular failure. Do this routine 4 times a week (e.g., Mon, Tue, Thu, Fri). It is designed to broaden shoulders, build legs, and fix posture for height.

🦍

Push-ups

Chest/Triceps

3 Sets to Failure

Rest 60 seconds between sets.

  • Keep core tight, body straight.
  • Go all the way down, chest touches floor.
  • If too hard, do them on your knees or hands elevated on a bed.

Bulgarian Split Squats

Legs/Glutes

3 Sets of 10-15 (Each Leg)

Rest 60 seconds between sets.

  • Put one foot back on a chair or bed.
  • Lower yourself until your back knee almost touches the ground.
  • Leg muscles are the biggest in the body; building them increases overall body weight fast.
